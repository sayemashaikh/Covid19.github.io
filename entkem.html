ENT

The ENT Unit (Ear Nose Throat) at KEM Hospital, Pune offers the full spectrum of diagnostic and treatment services. We offer 24 hours Emergency ENT services for ear aches, nose bleeds, foreign body removal and other services. Our ENT is supported by an excellent OT equipped with high tech instruments for ENT surgeries.

    OPD Services:-

  • Routine ENT examination
  • Ear microscopy
  • Wax removal
  • Diagnostic nasal endoscopy
  • Laryngoscopy
  • Audiometry and BERA for hearing evaluation
  • Hearing aids and rehabilitation
  • OAE for new-born hearing evaluation
  • Stroboscopy for voice analysis and speech therapy

    IPD Surgeries:-

  • Microscopic ear surgery for hearing improvement and disease removal

    Cochlear Implant and Implantable Hearing Devices and Rehablitiation:-

    Our neonatal and infant hearing screening program, ensures that every baby born at KEM Hospital undergoes hearing evaluation before leaving the hospital. Special attention is paid to children in the Neonatal Intensive Care Unit (NICU) as these premature, low birth weight babies are at a high risk to develop deafness. We offer early fitting of hearing aids and training of speech and language development as early as 3 months of age.
    The special ‘BIG EARS’ project is the first of its kind in India with a focus on early detection, diagnosis and therapy of hearing loss.
