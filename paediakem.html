Oral & Maxillofacial Surgery

The Department of Oral and Maxillofacial Surgery (OMFS) at KEM Hospital, Pune specialises in diagnosis and treatment of conditions and diseases affecting the head, neck and face region. Our highly trained Oral and Maxillofacial Surgeons are dedicated to patient welfare and are available round-the-clock.
In cases of road traffic accidents, our OMFS team forms an integral part of the first respondent team and plans the treatment accordingly. The surgeons collaborate with one another and with other specialists at KEMH to provide comprehensive care to patients. All treatments provided are cost effective and utilise advanced techniques and equipment, such as laser, piezosurgery and physiodispenser.
Our Oral and Maxillofacial Surgeons offer a wide range of services encompassing both OPD and IPD procedures.

    1. Oral Surgery:-

  • Oral manifestations of systemic diseases
  • Oral diagnosis and health screening
  • Mucosal disorders
  • Oral ulcers
  • Red and white lesions
  • Premalignant disease
  • Surgical extraction
  • Surgical exposure of impacted teeth
  • Peri apical infections and pathology
  • Frenectomy
  • Oro antral fistula repair
  • Transplantation of teeth
  • Removal or reduction of tori
  • Alveoloplasty
  • Genial tubercle reduction
  • Pre-prosthetic surgery
  • Mylohyoid ridge reduction
  • Vestibuloplast
  • Ridge augmentation
  • Ridge reduction
  • Bone grafts
