Endocrinology & Diabetology

Department of Endocrinology & Diabetes of DYPH offer comprehensive services in diagnosis, treatment and management of all hormonal disorders. Specialized clinics like obesity, bone health & thyroid are designed to provide comprehensive and state of art management.

SERVICES AVAILABLE

  • The department offers a broad range of services for diabetes, thyroid disorders, growth abnormalities in children, Juvenile and gestational diabetes, osteoporosis, pituitary disorders, obesity management, hormone replacement therapy and various other hormonal disorders.
  • We aim at early detection and active management of various complications like diabetic neuropathy, nephropathy, Ischemic heart diseases, retinopathy, sexual disorders and other endocrinal disorders like Pituitary, Adrenal Gland and Parathyroid Gland.
  • OPD Clinics is on Every Wednesday from 10.00 am to 12.00 noon and Follow up on Every Friday and Saturday.
