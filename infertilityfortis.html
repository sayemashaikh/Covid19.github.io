INFERTILITY MEDICINE

Infertility medicine team specializes in treating unexplained infertility, endometriosis and failed IUI. With our vast experience and expert team of clinicians, the Fortis Bloom IVF Centre specializes in treating patients who have had multiple failed IVF cycles. The Department follows individual protocols based on the patient's response. The department specializes in treating patients through multiple procedures including intrauterine insemination, surgical methods, medical or assisted yet advanced reproductive technologies. The team continuously strives to upgrade the medical protocols and adopt advanced technologies in order to attain the highest successful outcomes and emphasize its dedication to help families grow. The expert counselling and safe technology approach of the team has ensured successful results and enhanced satisfaction levels for many couples.
