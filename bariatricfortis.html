Bariatric Surgery

The Department of Bariatric & Metabolic Surgery helps to improve or resolve obesity-related diseases and conditions, including type 2 diabetes, heart disease, certain cancers, sleep apnea, GERD, high blood pressure, high cholesterol, sleep apnea, joint problems and depression. We consider obesity as a curable medical condition and hence provide a broad spectrum of support services. The Department is specialized in minimally invasive techniques such as robotic, laparoscopic and endoscopic procedures which enable minimal blood loss, less tissue damage, faster recovery, less post- operative follow-ups and minimal complications. We possess latest technologies which offer scar less minimal access surgery options to the patients. The skilled team has access to an extremely wide portfolio in this field of medicine and bariatric team of doctors performs all kinds of abdominal surgeries laparoscopically, which are typically performed using open surgery. The team also focuses on creating personalized plans considering patient health, weight loss goals, lifestyle and emotional issues in order to meet patient needs.The Department provides all the patients with comprehensive Bariatric and Metabolic surgery options and compassionate care, helping the patients (women and men) to achieve more active and healthy life. Resultantly, the patients encounter a remarkable improvement in their health.
