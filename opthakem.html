Ophthalmology

The Department of Ophthalmology at KEM Hospital, Pune provides comprehensive care for the treatment of conditions and diseases of the eyes. We have a well-equipped Out-Patient Department (OPD), setup with the latest equipment.
Our department has attained the status of postgraduate training centre in Ophthalmology. In addition to our clinics and camps, we have a deep interest in servicing the rural community in India. Our Vadu Rural Health Program (VRHP) that services several villages, performs numerous cataract surgeries.

Services

    Services

  • General ophthalmic evaluation
  • Diabetic retinopathy screening
  • ROP (Retinopathy of Prematurity) screening and treatment
  • Cataract services with different types of intraocular and premium lenses (Monofocal, Toric, Multifocal, Trifocal and EDOF)
  • Topical phacoemulsification services
  • Paediatric ophthalmic services
  • Cornea services
  • Vitreo-Retina service
  • Ophthalmic plastic and reconstructive services
  • Neuro ophthalmology services
  • Ophthalmic ultrasound
  • Ophthalmic visual field
  • Glaucoma diagnosis and management services
  • BOTOX treatment
