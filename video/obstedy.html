Cardiology

Cardiology

D Y patil Hospital offer the full range of cardiology services, from early disease detection to complex interventions. Our diagnostic services include ECG, echocardiography, stress test and angiography. The Cardiac Catheterization Laboratory, where angiographies, angioplasties etc. are performed, is a state-of-the-art, fully digital panel Cathlab from Siemens. The flat panel technology replaces the image intensifier technology, which has been in use for many years.

FACILITES AVAILABLE:

1. INTERVENTIONAL CARDIOLOGY:

  • Coronory Angiography
  • Coronory Angioplasty
  • Paces Maker Implantation (Temporaty / Permantant)
  • Baloon Valvo Plasties (Mitral / Aortic / Pulmonary)
  • Cardiac Cath Studies
  • Device Closures (ASD, PDA, VSD).
  • Peripheral Angiography & Angioplasty (Neck / Kidney / Limb)
  • Carotid Angiography & Angioplasty
  • EP Studies & Ablation.
  • Heart Failure Device and AICD implantation.

2. NON INVASIVE CARDIOLOGY :

  • 2D Echo Cardigraphy & Color Doppler Studies
  • Stress Testing
  • Holter Monitoring
  • Baloon Valvo Plasties (Mitral / Aortic / Pulmonary)
  • Contrast Echo Cardiography
  • Dobutamin Stress Test
