Urology

The Department of Urology at KEM Hospital, is a surgical specialty, which deals with diseases of the urinary and reproductive tracts in men and with urinary diseases in women.
The department was formally started with services like cystoscopy, Transurethral Resection of Prostate (TURP), bladder tumour and stenting, apart from open Urology for management of kidney stones. With a highly experienced team and the latest sophisticated equipment, we continue to provide support to patients in need and conduct several prostate and stone awareness camps for the benefit of patients.

    Services:-

  • Percutaneous Nephrostolithotomy (PNL)
  • Chronic Neutrophilic Leukaemia (CNL)
  • Ureteroscopy (URS)
  • Retrograde Intra Renal Surgery (RIRS)
  • Reconstructive urology
  • Female urology
  • Paediatric urology
  • Renal transplantation
  • Urological cancers
  • Andrology
  • Urological trauma
  • Kidney stones

About us
