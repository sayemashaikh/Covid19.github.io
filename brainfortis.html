Brain Surgery

Brain is a vital component of the body and controls various unconscious and conscious body functions. The proper functioning of brain is imperative to the entire body functions and any malfunction in brain might require a brain surgery. Some of the symptoms which can be an indicator of requirement of a brain surgery include Headache, Seizures, feeling of drowsiness or nausea and vomitting. Fortis is equipped to perform multiple types of brain surgery including surgeries related to multiple types of brain cancers such as Gliomas, Lymphomas, Chrodomas, Pituitary tumour, Medulloblastoma, Acoustic neuroma or schwannoma, Dysembryoplastic neuroepithelial tumour, Primitive neuroectodermal tumour etc. Fortis also conducts brain surgeries in conditions related to change in blood flow in the brain including conditions such as Stroke, Subdural haematoma, Subarachnoid haemorrhage etc. The other conditions which are treatment through brain surgeries at Fortis include Hydrocephalus, Brain infections, and multiple other malignancies related to the Brain. The Brain surgery is a very complex procedure and an expert team of Brain surgeons at Fortis ensures that the surgery is performed with complete precision. Also, the availability of most advanced equipments at Fortis ensures that the surgeries are performed using the latest technology and require minimum recovery time for the patient with the higher success rate.
