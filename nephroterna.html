Nephrology

Diseases like Diabetes, Hypertension and Chronic Glomerulonephritis can lead to the permanent loss of renal functions. The emergence of new therapeutic inventions has now made possible early diagnosis and the ability to control the renal disease.


Terna Speciality Hospital & Research Centre is a leading hospital for Nephrology treatments with expert Nephrologists and Urologists under one roof. A professionally managed unit with a team of dedicated doctors and paramedic staff, 24-hour care is provided to both inpatients and OPD patients.

    Services:-

  • Counselling and Kidney Disorder Management
  • Kidney Surgeries and Procedures
  • Kidney Biopsy
  • Dialysis
