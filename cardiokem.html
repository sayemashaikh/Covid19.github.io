Cardiology

KEM Hospital, Pune has a sophisticated Cardiac Catheterization Lab, where all interventional cardiac procedures, such as angiography, angioplasty, valvuloplasty (BMV, BPV) and pacemaker implantations are performed.
We also have a daily Cardiology OPD.
Our cardiologists and surgeons are highly trained and are experienced in diagnosing and treating both common and uncommon heart conditions. Our services are aimed at lowering peopleâ€™s risk for cardiovascular disease and its complications.

    The objective of the clinic is to curtail the ongoing epidemic of cardiovascular disease. Abnormal cholesterol and triglyceride levels increase the risk of heart attack, stroke and other cardiovascular diseases. Our clinic will help you to achieve normal cholesterol levels through a combination of lifestyle changes and medication.

    Services

  • Coronary Angiography and Angioplasty
  • Valvuloplasty - Percutaneous Balloon Mitral Valvuloplasty (BMV), Percutaneous Balloon Pulmonary Valvuloplasty (BPV)
  • Pacemaker Implantation - temporary and permanent
  • Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillator (ICD)
  • Device closures
  • Electrophysiology studies and RF Ablation
  • Intravascular imaging - IVUS, OCT
  • Rotational Atherectomy (RA)
  • Cardiac Resynchronization Therapy (CRT)
  • Cardiac 2D echo and colour doppler
  • Exercise stress test (TMT)
  • Transesophageal Echocardiography (TEE)
  • Ambulatory BP monitoring
  • Holter and long-term arrhythmia monitoring
  • Electrocardiogram (ECG or EKG)
  • Preventive cardiology and rehabilitation
  • 20 bedded ICCU (Intensive Coronary Care Unit) / Neuro ICU
  • Cardiology OPD
  • Full biochemical profile, including lipid profile
  • Nutritionist consult
