Cancer Cytogenetics

Department of Cancer Cytogenetics

    Cancer Cytogenetics Department comprises service program and research programs. Cancer Cytogenetics service program is extensively expanded to patients referred from TMH and other medical centers from India. Every year, around 6000 bone marrow / peripheral blood specimens are investigated in cancer patients at diagnosis and on treatment. The department provides comprehensive profile of tests in Multiple Myeloma (MM), Chronic Myeloid Leukemia (CML), Chronic Lymphocytic Leukemia (CLL), Acute Myeloid Leukemia (AML), Acute Lymphoblastic Leukemia (ALL), Myelodysplastic Syndromes (MDS), Myeloproliferative disorders (MPD) and Lymphomas. The department is NABL (National Accreditation Board for Laboratories) accredited and participates in External Quality Assessment program (EQAS) with College of American Pathologist (CAP). The laboratory staff is highly skilled team of Scientific Officers, Scientific Assistants, Technicians and supportive staff.
