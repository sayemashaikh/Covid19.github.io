Plastic Surgery

Creating and Giving the Best Smiles to Everyone

The Plastic Surgery Unit at KEM Hospital, Pune was established in 1971. Our team is committed to delivering optimal patient care using evidence-based medicine. We are important members of the KEM Hospital trauma team. We work closely and actively collaborate with physicians from other specialties, such as General Surgery, Orthopaedics, ENT, Ophthalmology, Paediatrics, Neurosurgery and Dermatology, to offer patients comprehensive medical care to meet their reconstructive and cosmetic surgical needs.
