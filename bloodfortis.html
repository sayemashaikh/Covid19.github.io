Blood Disorders

The department of Hematology deals in the management of blood-related disorders. In case, there is any problem in the bloodstream, it can affect the overall health of a person. The team of hematology expertises in providing an accurate diagnosis of the problem and its treatment. There are various types of blood disorders and blood cancers that can affect a person's well-being. The team at Fortis, specializes in handling such problems with perfection. Some of the bleeding related disorders treated at Fortis include Von Willebrand's disease, hemophilia, blood clots, enzyme deficiencies, Iron metabolism disorders, anemia, myeloid disorders and blood cancers like lymphoma, myeloma and leukemia. The department usually deals with the problems affecting lymph nodes, bone marrow, red and white blood cells, blood vessels, platelets, proteins involved in clotting and bleeding (thrombosis and hemostasis). The department is well-equipped with all the advanced technologies and the staff is specialized in practicing the latest and efficient techniques to provide the best patient care. The department offers various diagnostic services as well, including MRI, Ultrasound, X-Ray, CT-Scans, PET scan, etc. The patient safety, comfort and satisfaction are the main priorities for the team. The hematology team makes sure to deliver definite, detailed and on time laboratory test reports to the patients.
