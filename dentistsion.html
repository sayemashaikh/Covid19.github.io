The department of Dentistry was started on 1st February 1956, thanks to the initiative of the then superintendent Dr. S.V. Joglekar. The department was headed by Dr.R.B.Raut, Honorary Dental Surgeon, he was assisted by Dr. B.R. Rao, Honorary Assistant Dental Surgeon, He later took over the charge of the department, Dr.V.K.Gandhi later joined the department as Honorary Assistant Dental Surgeon. Subsequently Dr.P.M.Shanbhag took over as Honorary Dental Surgeon & Head of the Dental department .

About us

Lokmanya Tilak Municipal Medical College and General Hospital is a full-fledged Government medical college located in Mumbai, Maharashtra. The college imparts the degree Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery (MBBS). It is one of the most prestigious medical colleges in India. It is recognised by the Medical Council of India. This is one of the oldest medical colleges in Mumbai. Lokmanya Tilak municipal medical college hospital is also locally known as Sion Hospital
