OPHTHALMOLOGY

Department OF OPHTHALMOLOGY

At DYPH , Ophthalmology is the branch of medicine which deals with the diseases and surgery of the visual pathways, including the eye, brain, and areas surrounding the eye, such as the lacrimal system and eyelids.
