General Medicine

The Department of General Medicine at KEM Hospital, Pune aims at providing comprehensive care to patients encompassing various specialties. We are dedicated to managing all aspects of the general health care of adults. The strength of our department is the wisdom and experience of our doctors and the enthusiasm to create niche specialties.
We pioneered renal transplants in the city with the expansion of the extensive nephrology unit, introduced radiological intervention in neurology, run one of the most pocket friendly Intensive Care Units (ICUs) and are now advancing to help in the prevention of cardiovascular disease with the introduction of the Lipid Clinic.

    Lipid Clinic:-

    The objective of the clinic is to curtail the ongoing epidemic of cardiovascular disease. Abnormal cholesterol and triglyceride levels increase the risk of heart attack, stroke and other cardiovascular diseases. Our clinic will help you to achieve normal cholesterol levels through a combination of lifestyle changes and medication.

    1. Our Goals

  • Primary Prevention: Reducing the risk of development of cardiovascular diseases by early identification and management of risk factors
  • Secondary Prevention: Lowering the risk of future events, for those with established coronary artery disease, strokes and peripheral artery disease
  • Diagnosis and management of genetically determined lipid disorders in the index patient and other family members
  • Management of associated metabolic problems

    2. Services

  • Diagnostics and lab screening for abnormal cholesterol levels
  • Management of raised lipid values
  • Genetic testing
  • Counselling on risk factors caused by increased cholesterol levels
  • Counselling for lifestyle modification
