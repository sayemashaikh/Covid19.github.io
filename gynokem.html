Obstetrics & Gynaecology

The Department of Obstetrics and Gynaecology at KEM Hospital, Pune offers a wide range of comprehensive services for the care of women. We are a tertiary care, center of excellence in Obstetrics, Gynaecology, Urogynaecology, Oncology and Infertility.

    Our Accomplishments:

  • Growth disorders, such as short stature and obesity
  • We have been recognised as a referral centre for pregnancies at high risk and complications, within Pune and the rest of Maharashtra, with the support of our blood bank, excellent neonatology set up, and intensive care units
  • A majority of cases of Morbidly Adherent Placenta, a condition that is increasing day-by-day, are referred to our department
  • Puberty disorders, such as delayed and precious (occurring too early) puberty
  • We developed a new surgical technique in the field of uterine prolapse, which has been accepted internationally and has been followed by many consultants in India
  • We have successfully organized conferences and workshops, which are attended by many Ob-Gyn specialists from all over Maharashtra, for over 20 years
  • We have contributed in publishing several research articles in standard international journals with high impact factor

    1. Obstetric Care:-

  • Out-patient services
  • Preconception counselling and prenatal diagnosis
  • Streamlined rational antenatal care with thorough screening of foetal and maternal conditions
  • Expertise in the management of pregnancy complications including PIH, foetal growth restriction, preterm labour with backup of neonatal intensive care provided by a 40-bedded, state-of-the-art level III Neonatal ICU
  • Multi-disciplinary management of medical disorders such as diabetes, heart disease, liver disease and critical illnesses in pregnancy
  • A referral centre for severe obstetric emergencies such as postpartum haemorrhage, liver disease, eclampsia and other hypertensive emergencies
  • An Obstetric ICU within the Labour and Delivery suite to manage these cases

    2. High Risk Obstetrics:-

  • Morbidly Adherent Placenta and management of PPH
  • Ectopic pregnancies
  • Eclampsia and preeclampsia
  • Pregnancy associated with diabetes
  • Elder mother (> 35 years)
  • A referral centre for severe obstetric emergencies such as postpartum haemorrhage, liver disease, eclampsia and other hypertensive emergencies
  • Breech presentation
  • Grand multipara
  • All major medical disorders
  • Previous 3rd stage abnormalities / problems
  • Multiple pregnancy
  • All malpresentations
  • Cephalopelvic Disproportion (CPD)
  • Previous Lower Segment Caesarean Section (LSCS)
  • All hypertensive disorders
  • Recurrent premature labour
  • Rh negative women with Rh positive husbands
  • Gynaecological abnormality
  • Elderly primi
  • History of infertility
  • Extremes of age, regardless of parity
  • History of abortions

    3. General Gynaecology:-

  • Out-patient services: Diagnosis and management of gynaecological problems like menstrual disorders, genital infections, infertility, preventive screening for and evaluation of genital malignancies
  • Family Planning Clinic: Contraception and medical termination of pregnancy

    4. Family Planning:-

    The Department of Family Planning at KEM Hospital, Pune offers various family welfare services. We believe that a planned pregnancy contributes towards a better health outcome. Family planning enables people to improve pregnancy planning, such as their desired number of children and spacing of pregnancies, make informed choices and prevent unintended pregnancy.

    Services:

  • Family planning counselling and motivation to patients
  • Helping patients choose the appropriate method of contraception
  • Providing access to the various methods of contraception, including oral pills, injectable contraceptives, intrauterine devices, and permanent methods of sterilization, such as tubal ligation and vasectomy
  • Counselling and advice on Medical Termination of Pregnancy
  • Immunization in pregnancy
  • Vaccination and child immunizations
  • Participation in the National Pulse polio programme and other programmes offered by the government

    5. Gynaecological Surgery:-

  • Hysterectomy: Abdominal, Vaginal, Non Descent Vaginal Hysterectomy (NDVH)
  • Gynaec Oncosurgery: Radical hysterectomy and debulking surgery with facilities for medical oncology

    6. Gynaecological Oncology:-

    Gynaecological malignancies like Ca Cervix, Ca Endometrium and ovarian cancers are managed with help from other specialities whenever required.

    7. Urogynaecology:-

    The department of Obstetrics and Gynaecology provides excellent services to women suffering from urogynaecological disorders such as uterine and vaginal prolapse, stress urinary incontinence and urinary fistula. Burch colposuspension, a gold standard in urinary stress incontinence, has been pioneered in the department and has been practiced for over 30 years with excellent patient satisfaction rates.
