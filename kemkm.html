The Promoters

Dr. BANOO COYAJI - FORMER CHIEF MEDICAL OFFICER

Much of the growth was spearheaded by the dynamic and visionary of Dr. Banoo Coyaji, who was appointed as the Cheif Medical Officer in 1944 and was awarded the Padma Bhushan and Magsaysay Awards. The hospital is particularly known for some of its Centers of Excellence- many of which were the first of their kind in pune and the surrounding region. These include Critical Care, Diabetology, Nephrology and Dialysis, High Risk Obstetrics, Neonatology and a Comprehensive Rehabiliitation Centre for children with special needs. At Dr. Banoo Coyaji's behest, the hospital set up its Department of Medical Social Work (MSW), one of the earliest in Maharashtra, to look into the problems faced by patients and their families.
