Neuro Surgery

Neuro Surgery

The Unit of Neuro Surgery at KEM Hospital, Pune began in 1970. Our unit provides state-of-the art neurosurgery services for patients with disorders of the brain, spine and peripheral nerve pathways, and for patients with traumatic skull, brain and spinal cord injuries.
We work collaboratively with neurologists, pain management specialists, and nurses to manage the complex care of neurosurgery patients. Our consultants also teach the resident doctors and nurses during ward rounds and operative sessions.

    Services

  • Head injuries
  • Spinal tumours and injuries
  • Brain tumours
  • Slipped discs
  • Intracranial aneurysm
  • Vascular malformations
  • Various neurological pain conditions

About us

With about 390 staff physicians and 550 resident doctors, the 1800 bedded hospital treats about 1.8 million out-patients and 85,000 in-patients annually and provides both basic care and advanced treatment facilities in all fields of medicine and surgery.
